Catherine J. Randall - Director since 2015. Dr. Randall, 69, is Chairman of the Board of Pettus Randall Holdings, Inc. (a real estate company), in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, a position she has held since 2002. She is the former Chairman of the Board of Randall Publishing Company and a former Director of the University Honors Program at the University of Alabama. Dr. Randall serves on the Board of Advisors of privately-held Mercedes-Benz U.S. International, Inc. She has served as National President of Mortar Board, Inc., President of the Board of Directors of the Alabama Women's Hall of Fame, Director of Alabama Girls State, and Chair of the American Village Board of Directors. Dr. Randall's dedication to the State of Alabama and experience in the business and academic communities make her a valuable member of the Company's Board.
